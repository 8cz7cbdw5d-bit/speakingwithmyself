# Speaking with Myself - Version 1.1 Release Notes

## What's New

We've listened to your feedback and made some thoughtful improvements to help you build a more consistent reflection practice.

### 🔔 Notifications Now Enabled by Default

We want to help you build the habit of daily reflection. Notifications are now enabled by default (9 AM) to gently remind you to check in with yourself. Don't worry - you can customize the time or turn them off anytime in Settings.

**First-time users:** You'll see a one-time message explaining this change.

### ⏰ Optional "Time to Write" Reminder

Sometimes you see the question in the morning but want to write later. Now you can set a second daily reminder specifically for writing time (disabled by default). Perfect for evening reflection!

**How to enable:** Settings → Notifications → Time to Write

### 🎯 Keep Questions Until You're Ready

Some questions deserve more time. Enable "Retain Question Until Completed" in Settings to keep the same question until you answer it, instead of advancing daily. Great for deeper topics that need extra reflection.

**How it works:** 
- Question stays until you respond
- No pressure to answer every day
- Your pace, your journey

### ⚡ Power Through When Inspired

Feeling the flow? After answering a question, tap "Next Question" to continue your journey without waiting for tomorrow. Perfect for those moments when insights are flowing and you want to keep the momentum going.

**Look for:** The green "Next Question" button after saving your reflection

### 📊 Better History Context

Your History now shows when you actually answered each question, not just when it was asked. This gives you better context about your reflection journey, especially if you're working ahead or taking your time with questions.

### 🎨 Polish & Improvements

- Cleaner progress indicators (1-7 instead of truncated labels)
- Better text editor experience (save button always visible)
- Smoother progression through your 7-day cycles
- More intuitive "working ahead" indicator

## What Stays the Same

- All your existing reflections are preserved
- Complete privacy - everything stays on your device
- No account required
- Same thoughtful 7-day reflection cycles
