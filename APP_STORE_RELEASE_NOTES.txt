What's New in Version 1.1

We've made thoughtful improvements to help you build a consistent reflection practice:

• Notifications enabled by default - Gentle daily reminders to check in with yourself (customize or disable in Settings)

• Optional "Time to Write" reminder - Set a second notification for when you're ready to reflect (great for evening writing!)

• Keep questions until ready - New setting to retain questions until you answer them, instead of advancing daily

• Power through when inspired - Tap "Next Question" to continue your journey when insights are flowing

• Better history context - See when you actually answered each question for clearer reflection tracking

• Polish & improvements - Cleaner progress indicators, better text editor, smoother experience

All your existing reflections are preserved and safe. Everything stays private on your device.

Thank you for showing up for yourself every day.
